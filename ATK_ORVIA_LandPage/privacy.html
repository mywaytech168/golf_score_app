The information the Service Provider requests will be retained and used as described in this privacy policy.

Camera, Microphone, and Swing Recordings

ORVIA is a golf swing analysis application. With your permission, the Application accesses your device's camera to record swing videos and your microphone to detect ball-impact sounds used for automatic swing clipping. Recordings are stored locally on your device. When you choose to run a cloud analysis, the selected video and/or derived motion data (such as body pose keypoints and ball trajectory data) are uploaded to the Service Provider's servers for processing.

You can deny or revoke camera and microphone permissions at any time in your device settings; doing so will disable the recording features but not the rest of the Application.

Artificial Intelligence

The Application uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to provide its core features:

  • On-device processing: body pose estimation and ball detection run locally on your device.
  • Cloud processing: when you request an AI swing analysis, your swing video and/or derived motion data are transmitted to the Service Provider's servers and may be processed by third-party AI services (such as Google Gemini) to generate analysis results and coaching feedback.

AI-generated analysis is provided for informational and training purposes only. All AI processing is performed in accordance with this privacy policy and applicable laws. If you have questions about the AI features or data processing, please contact the Service Provider.
